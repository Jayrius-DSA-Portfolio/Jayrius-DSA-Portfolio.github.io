P5 NRPC

In this competition, we had to code a robot that could tackle the missions in the competition, such as the Ferris wheel, the generator, the bell, and many more. I learned how to quickly adapt to mission changes, known as surprise rules that are given on the competition day. Our robot would navigate the field with the lines, and use attachments like a hammer that would slam down on the bell and Ferris wheel, a status cage to move the food store, and the bumper car around. Our robot was compact so that it would not have wires sticking out affecting the performance of the run. This was also to ensure that the robot had no obstructions and did not knock down the walls of the map when turning or line tracing.
