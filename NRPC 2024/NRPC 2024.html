NRPC 2024

During NRPC 2024, our team faced a challenging yet rewarding task that required both strategic planning and technical expertise. The competition setup involved navigating various obstacles, including 3 virus props, 1 data block, 1 Wi-Fi security system, 3 ad blockers, 12 firewalls, and 1 glitch wall. These firewalls and the glitch wall acted as impassable barriers, adding complexity to our task. Our objectives were clear: move the 3 virus props to the anti-virus zone, transport the data block to the data collection center, push a button to activate the Wi-Fi security, and push the ad blockers down.
Throughout the competition, I gained a deeper understanding of gyro capabilities and refined my skills in path planning, problem-solving, and robot navigation. The experience also allowed me to improve my debugging abilities. Our team's effective collaboration and persistence through trial and error led us to not only overcome the obstacles but also emerge victorious by seizing first place.
